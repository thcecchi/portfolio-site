About

In 2014 I left my job as an account guy at an ad agency to pursue a career developing awesome digital experiences. I firmly believe that creating a great experience for people is the best marketing a brand can have. Experiences have to power to create conversation, action, loyalty and evangalism. They have the power to bring brands to life and become a part of people's lives.

My advertising roots force me to develop people-first products with empathy for end user, while my insatiable thirst for learning and creating keeps me consistently pushing myself to create more beautiful interfaces, more powerful interactions, and more technically savvy products.

I want to create awesome experiences with awesome products for awesome people.
