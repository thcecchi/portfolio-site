About

Hey! I'm Tyler

I'm a former big agency account guy who decided to become a front-end/UI developer and pursue a career developing awesome digital products and experiences, because I believe that great brand experiences are the best marketing. Great experiences have to power to create conversation, action, loyalty and evangalism. They have the power to bring brands to life and to become a part of people's lives.

My advertising background helps me develop people-first products with empathy for the end-user, while my insatiable appetite for learning and creating constantly pushes me to create more beautiful interfaces, more powerful interactions, and more technically sophisticated products.

I simply want to create awesome experiences with awesome products for awesome people.
